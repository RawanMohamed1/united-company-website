Ladders

Manufacturing of industrial ladders, safety ladders, and access equipment for various applications.

Overview

United Company ANA specializes in manufacturing high-quality industrial ladders and safety access equipment. We produce various types of ladders including fixed ladders, portable ladders, extension ladders, and safety ladders. All our ladders are designed and manufactured to meet international safety standards and are suitable for industrial, commercial, and maintenance applications.

Features

  • Industrial ladders - Heavy-duty ladders for industrial use
  • Safety ladders - OSHA compliant safety equipment
  • Access equipment - Fixed and portable access solutions
  • Custom ladder fabrication - Tailored to your specifications
  • Extension ladders - Adjustable height solutions
  • Fixed ladders - Permanent installation solutions
  • Safety cages and platforms - Complete safety systems

Applications

Our ladders are used in warehouses, factories, maintenance facilities, construction sites, storage tanks, silos, and various industrial installations. All ladders are manufactured from high-quality materials including stainless steel, aluminum, and carbon steel, ensuring durability, safety, and long-term performance.
