Piping Systems

Installation of all types of pipes including fire networks, steam systems, and central air conditioning.

Overview

United Company ANA provides comprehensive piping system installation services for various industrial and commercial applications. We install all types of pipes including fire protection networks, steam systems, central air conditioning, dairy pipelines, pharmaceutical pipelines, and petroleum pipelines. Our team also provides drilling services using core drills for precise installations.

Features

  • Fire protection systems - Complete fire network installation
  • Steam pipe networks - High-pressure steam systems
  • HVAC piping - Central air conditioning systems
  • Industrial pipeline installation - For various industrial processes
  • Dairy and pharmaceutical pipelines - Sanitary piping systems
  • Petroleum pipelines - Specialized oil and gas systems
  • Core drilling services - Precise hole drilling for installations

Applications

Our piping systems serve various industries including food processing, pharmaceuticals, petrochemicals, HVAC systems, fire safety, and industrial manufacturing. All installations comply with international standards and local regulations, ensuring safety, efficiency, and long-term reliability.
