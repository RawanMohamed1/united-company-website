Steel Structures

Manufacturing of iron trusses, crystal works, factory fences, barriers, and flooring equipment.

Overview

United Company ANA specializes in manufacturing steel structures including iron trusses, crystal structural works, factory fences, barriers, and flooring equipment. We design and fabricate custom steel structures for industrial, commercial, and architectural applications. Our structures are engineered for strength, durability, and aesthetic appeal.

Features

  • Iron trusses fabrication - Structural support systems
  • Crystal structural works - Decorative and functional steel structures
  • Factory fencing - Security and boundary solutions
  • Industrial barriers - Safety and traffic control barriers
  • Flooring equipment - Mezzanine floors and platforms
  • Ladders and handrails - Safety access solutions
  • Custom design and fabrication - Tailored to your specifications

Applications

Our steel structures are used in warehouses, factories, commercial buildings, industrial facilities, architectural projects, and infrastructure development. All structures are designed according to engineering standards and building codes, ensuring safety, stability, and long-term performance.
