Tanks & Reservoirs

Manufacturing of all types of tanks including stainless steel, double jacket, single, and carbon steel tanks.

Overview

United Company ANA specializes in manufacturing high-quality tanks and reservoirs for various industrial applications. We produce stainless steel tanks, double jacket tanks, single tanks, carbon steel tanks, silos, and heat exchangers. Our tanks are designed and manufactured to meet the highest quality standards for food, pharmaceutical, chemical, and industrial applications.

Features

  • Stainless steel tanks - High-quality 304/316 stainless steel construction
  • Double jacket tanks - For temperature-controlled applications
  • Single tanks - Standard storage solutions
  • Carbon steel tanks - For industrial applications
  • Heat exchangers - Efficient thermal transfer systems
  • Silos - Large-scale storage solutions
  • Custom fabrication - Tailored to your specifications

Applications

Our tanks and reservoirs are used in food processing, pharmaceutical manufacturing, chemical industries, dairy processing, beverage production, and various industrial applications. All tanks are manufactured according to international standards and can be customized to meet specific capacity, pressure, and temperature requirements.
